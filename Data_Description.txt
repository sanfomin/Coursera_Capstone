The data about collisions is provided by SPD and recorded by Traffic Records. The dataset includes information about 194,673 collisions of all types in 
Seattle from beginning of 2014 to May of 2020.
For each collision the dataset contains coordinates of the incident, its severity, number of people and cars involved, incident date and time, weather 
and road condition, whether or not a driver involved was under the influence of drugs or alcohol.
